Welcome

This friendly group runs on Tuesday mornings (term time only) from 9.30-11.30am. There are lots of toys, space to run around, a craft activity and song time each week.

We have lots of special events at various times in the year such as a music group, “Shake, Rattle and Roll”, pottery painting, seasonal parties, messy play and a local photographer. It's a great way for parents/carers and their children to meet each other in a relaxed and fun atmosphere.

It's priced at £2.50 per child, 50p for additional children. A drink and snack is also included for both adults and children.
