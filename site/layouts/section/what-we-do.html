What We Do

FREE PLAY

We have a wide variety of toys including cars, play kitchen, train sets, dolls, dressing up, Happy Land, giant Jenga, Skuttle Bugs, baby toys and mats, books, puzzles, etc for the children to use for free play.

CRAFT & MESSY PLAY

Each week we have a craft activity for the children to engage with. From time to time we have a session dedicated to Messy Play with several craft activities running together.

SINGING

Whilst the toys are being tidied away, we wrap up each session with a 15min group singing session. The children can make use of various musical instruments to play along as well as singing.

REFRESHMENTS

We have tea, coffee and biscuits for the adults, and the children are offered water, juice, fruit and biscuits.
